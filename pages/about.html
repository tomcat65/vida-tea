Our Story

The Beginning

Erika and Mercedes met through their shared passion for wellness and community. Erika's medical background and Mercedes' cultural heritage created the perfect foundation for a unique tea business.

The Vision

They envisioned a tea company that would serve everyone, not just a specific demographic. "Tea for Life" became their guiding principle - wellness teas accessible to all people, everywhere.

The Unique Approach

Recognizing the gap in the market, they focused on custom gift arrangements as their unique differentiator. Handcrafted by the founders themselves, these arrangements became their signature offering.

The Future

Starting in Katy, TX, but with universal appeal, Vida-Tea is positioned to grow beyond local boundaries while maintaining the personal touch that makes each arrangement special.
